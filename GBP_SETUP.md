## 1. Claim & Verify
1. Go to **https://business.google.com** and sign in with the clinic's Google account.
2. Search for "Sree Kayakalpam Vaidyashala, Mampoyil, Chunkathara". If it exists, click **Claim this business**; otherwise **Add your business**.
3. Enter the exact name: **Sree Kayakalpam Vaidyashala**.
4. Choose verification (postcard to the address, phone, or video). Complete it — the listing is not public until verified.

- Reply to every review (thank positives, address concerns politely). Target 4.5★+.
